Portable binaries bundled with this release:

  ffmpeg        macOS builds: static, from https://ffmpeg.martin-riedl.de
                Windows builds: BtbN GPL win64 build,
                sources at https://github.com/BtbN/FFmpeg-Builds
                FFmpeg is licensed under the GPL; sources at
                https://ffmpeg.org — these are unmodified upstream binaries.

It is redistributed here purely so the extractor's movie conversion works
on a fresh machine with zero additional installs. Replace the contents of
this tools/ folder with your own preferred build at any time; the kit
also picks up any ffmpeg on PATH or in common install locations.
